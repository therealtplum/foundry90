# Foundry90 Code of Conduct

Foundry90 is intended to be a welcoming, inclusive, and supportive space for learners and engineers at all levels.

To ensure that everyone has a positive experience, all contributors and participants agree to:

---

## 1. Respect Others  
- No harassment, insults, discrimination, or demeaning behavior  
- Assume good intent from others  
- Be patient with learners of all skill levels  

---

## 2. Be Constructive  
- Offer helpful feedback  
- Avoid gatekeeping  
- Focus on clarity and improvement  

---

## 3. Stay Professional  
- Communicate clearly  
- Avoid disruptive behavior  
- Keep discussions technical and respectful  

---

## 4. Protect the Community  
If you observe unacceptable behavior:
- Inform a maintainer  
- Provide context and examples  

Maintainers may remove content or contributors that violate these standards.

---

## 5. Compliance  
By interacting with this repo, you agree to this Code of Conduct.  
Serious violations may result in revoked access or removal of contributions.

---

If you have any concerns, please reach out to the maintainers privately.
